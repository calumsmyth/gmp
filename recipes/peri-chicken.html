Peri-Peri Chicken and Halloumi Rice

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:

  • 600g Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Peri-Peri Seasoning - More seasoning = more spice
  • 2 Tablespoons Tomato Puree
  • 1 Tablespoon Paprika
  • 400ml Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Chicken Stock Pot
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 Cup Uncooked Rice
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of Peri-Peri Mayonase
  • 1 Jalepeno (Optional)
  • 150g Halloumi (Optional)
