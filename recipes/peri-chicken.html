Peri-Peri Chicken and Halloumi Rice

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:
  • 600g Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Peri-Peri Seasoning - More seasoning = more spice
  • 2 Tablespoons Tomato Puree
  • 1 Tablespoon Paprika
  • 400ml Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Chicken Stock Pot
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 1 Cup Uncooked Rice
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of Peri-Peri Mayonase
  • 1 chopped Jalepeno (Optional)
  • 150g fried Halloumi (Optional)

Steps

Step 1

Fry chicken on medium-high heat with onion, garlic, red pepper and stir through peri-peri seasoning and paprika. Fry for approximately 8 minutes. Ensuring chicken is cooked throughout.

Step 2

Add uncooked rice and tomatoe puree to the pan and stir until mixed. Fry for a couple more minutes until you can smell the rice.

Step 3

Add chopped tomatoes and chicken stock to pan and stir together. Reduced heat on pan to a simmer. Leave to simmer until rice is cooked, rice packaging will often provide information on how long you can expect this to take.

Step 4

Add and stir through mixed vegetables.

Step 5 - (Optional) -

If you are adding fried Halloumi and/or Jalepeno you can either top with these to serve, or mix them through

Step 6

Either top with Peri-Peri mayonase to serve, or mix through.

Serve and Enjoy!
