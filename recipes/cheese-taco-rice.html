Cheesey Taco Rice

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:
  • Fry Light or 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • Taco seasoning
  • 2 beef stock cube - use enough water for 2 cubes as per instructions
  • 300g rice (dry weight)
  • 30g Tomate Puree
  • 60g Grated Cheese

Steps

Step 1

Add fry light/ Olive oil and diced onion to a large frying pan. Fry on medium heat until onions start to turn golden.

Step 2

As the onions begin to golden, add in beef mince and taco seasoning. Ensure to break up beef in pan with a wooden spoon and cook until meat is browned.

Step 3

Once beef is browned, add dry rice, beef stock and tomatoe puree. Cover with a lid and leave to simmer for about 10-12 minutes, until rice has become fluffy and cooked.

Step 4

(This step is optional, if you are aiming to cut down on fats, you may want to consider skipping this step) - Cover with grated cheese. Replace lid and leave to cook for a few more minutes until cheese is melted.

Step 5

Serve and Enjoy! You can eat it on its own, or for that full taco experience, can be eaten with tortila chips, in a tortilla or in a wrap
