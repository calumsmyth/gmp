Chicken & Chorizo Jambalaya

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 2 Chopped Chicken Breasts
  • 75g Sliced Chorizo
  • 400g Can Plum Tomatoe
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 1 Thinly Sliced Red Pepper
  • 2 Crushed Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tablespoon Cajun Seasoning
  • 250g Long Grain Rice
  • 350ml Chicken Stock

Steps

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a large frying pan and brown chopped chicken breasts for about 5-8 mins until golden.

Step 2

Remove chicken and set it aside. Add in onions and cook for furhter 3-4 mins until soft.

Step 3

Add red pepper, garlic, Chorizo and Cajun seasoning. Cook for further 5 mins.

Step 4

Add chicken back in with rice. Add tomatoes and chicken stock. Cover and let simmer for approximately 20-25 mins until rice is tender and appears cooked.

Serve and Enjoy!
