Sausage & Cannelini Bean Ragu

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive or Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Oregano
  • Pinch of Black Pepper
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • 6 Pork Sausages with the meat squeezed out from the skins
  • 2 Chopped Onions
  • 2 Chopped Garlic Cloves
  • 400g Chopped Plum Tomatoes
  • 400g Cannellini beans
  • 150ml of Beef Stock
  • 200g Spaghetti

Steps

Step 1

Heat oil in a frying pan on medium heat. Fry sausage meat until browned and fat has been released (approximately 6-7 mins). Ensuring to break up the meat with a spoon as you stir. Remove the meat and place on kitchen paper. Leave fat in the pan

Step 2

Fry the onions in the fat until softened (approximately 5 mins). Then add garlic, oregano and black pepper. Cover and fry until completely softened (approximately 6 mins).

Step 3

Return Sausage meat to the pan. Add tomatoes, salt and sugar. Then add beef stock. Simmer on low heat until the sauce has thickened slightly (approximately 15 mins).

Step 4

Add the beans with their liquid and cook until sauce has thickened slightly once more (approximately 10-15 mins)

Step 5

At the same time as the above step, cook spaghetti as per pack instructions. Combine the two once both cooked.

Serve and Enjoy!
