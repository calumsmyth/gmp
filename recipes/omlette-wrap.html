High Protien Omelette Wraps

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients: (The below recipe is per serving. Multiply ingredients by number of meals you would like to prepare.)
  • Knob of Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Slice of Ham, Shredded
  • Grated cheese
  • Splash of Milk/ Cream
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of Pepper
  • High Protein Wrap
  • Brown Sauce (Or sauce of personal choice)

Steps

Step 1

Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper and whisk. Once mixed, add milk/cream, ham and cheese and whisk once more.

Step 2

Add butter to a frying pan and heat on medium-high heat. Once pan is hot, add egg mixture to cover bottom of pan. Cook until underside begins to turn dark brown, using a spatular to lift edges and monitor progress. Once underside starts browning, flip onto other side.

Step 3

Heat wrap in the microwave for approximately 10-15 seconds, or follow instructions on package. Apply brown sauce to topside of wrap.

Step 4

Once the otherside of the omlette is cooked, place from pan onto wrap. Fold both sides in towards the center before folding bottom upwards.

Serve and Enjoy!
