Mince Ragu with Mixed Vegetables

Ingredients

For this recipe we will need the following ingredients:
  • 1 Tablespoon Rapssed Oil
  • 2 Sliced Onions
  • 3 Grated Garlic Cloves
  • 3 Finely Chopped Carrots
  • 200g of Mixed Forzen Vegetables
  • 500g steak mince
  • 1 Tablespoon Thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground pepper
  • Vegetable Stock Cube
  • 500g passata

Steps

Step 1

Heat the oil in a large non-stick pan on a medium-low heat. Fry the onions for 10 minutes until golden.

Step 2

Add the garlic and mince and cook until mince is browned. Ensure to break up mince and stir as it cooks.

Step 3

Add stock cube, passata, carrots, pepper and thyme. Cover and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally until veg is tender and meat is cooked.

Step 4

Add and stir through mixed vegetables for approximately 5 minutes until cooked.

Step 5

Serve with rice and Enjoy!
